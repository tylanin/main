The world is really cool and I try to understand it by writing about stuff that inspires curiosity.

I primarily cover culture, current affairs, and the arts. I'm particularly interested in subcultures and social media. Most of my articles are about Singapore, where I'm currently based in, but I'm interested in issues related to East Asia and Southeast Asia in general.

Sometimes I write at a more casual space on the internet.

I am fluent in English and Mandarin, used to learn French, and am learning Japanese and Korean.

When not writing, I can be found at the dance studio, practising ollies, running, planning my next trip, learning some game dev, or dabbling in other creative pursuits.
