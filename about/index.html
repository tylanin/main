I'm currently an editorial intern at Rice Media. I'm also a freelance writer, aspiring journalist, and struggling millennial. In a previous life, I was a linguist.

The world is really cool and I try to understand it by writing about stuff that gets me excited and inspires curiosity.

I primarily cover culture and current affairs. I'm particularly interested in subcultures, social media, and the arts. Most of my articles are about Singapore, where I'm currently based in, but I'm interested in issues related to East Asia and Southeast Asia in general.

I am fluent in English and Mandarin, used to learn French, and am learning Japanese and Korean.

When not writing, I can be found at the dance studio, practising ollies, running, planning my next trip, furiously editing this website, or dabbling in other creative pursuits.
