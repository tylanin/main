I am currently a linguistics research project officer with the Linguistics and Multilingual Studies program at the Nanyang Technological University in Singapore.

My research interests lie primarily in language variation, identity construction, and multilingualism. Specifically, I am interested in Singapore's migrant communities and youth subcultures, and utilize both quantitative and qualitative methods in my work.

For my Honors Thesis, I investigated the rhythm of Singapore English, Mandarin, and English-Mandarin code-switching and how it varies according to social class.
