My research interests lie primarily in language variation, sociophonetics, and multilingualism. Specifically, I am interested in Singapore's migrant communities and youth subcultures. I currently work with English, Mandarin, and Singlish.

For my Honors Thesis, I investigated the rhythm of Singapore English, Mandarin, and English-Mandarin code-switching and how it varies according to social class.
