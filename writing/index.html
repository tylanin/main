📝 WRITING

Previously, I was with TODAY, KrASIA, and RICE Media. I was also a freelance journalist/writer from 2016 to 2021.

I covered multiple beats, including (but not limited to) culture, tech, current affairs, and sustainability. On top of writing about Singapore, I also wrote about Southeast Asia and China. I'm comfortable interviewing profiles in Mandarin and translating between English and Mandarin. I have worked with multiple formats, such as longform features, press releases, blog posts, whitepapers, and op-eds.

I have worked with Atlas Obscura, PR Newswire Asia, RADII China, What's On Weibo, Meantime Magazine, and more.
